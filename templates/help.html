Uploading a Video

The upload buttons are circled in red above. The Title & video is required and a thumbnail will be auto-generated if one is not uploaded. Hashtags can go in the description box. Please allow time for the video to upload, do not leave the page especially if it is a large file.

Three Vertical Dots ⋮

Three video dots usually indicate to delete, edit, add or perform any other actions that may be added later on, the add button is for adding a video to one of your playlist. Three dots can apply to videos, playlist, comment, shares.

Playlist

You can create a playlist, then add videos to the playlist. This is only possible for now on the main video page for now, not from viewing a list of videos.

Share Feed

You can also display the website in a feed like style. This feed contains videos shared from the people you follow and yourself. By clicking the links in the red circle, you can access this feed.

Hashtags

Hashtags will only work in the description of a video, you can see a list of hashtags and search for them as well. Clicking a hashtag will result in showing a list of videos that contain that hashtag in the video. Hashtags are case insensitive.

Search

You can search for playlist, videos, hashtags, and in the categories section.

Edit Profile & Account

Edit Profile & Account settings such as profile pic, banner, & about. Also change your username, email, and password.

Notifications

Unfortunately for now, you must click any notification from the dropdown then it will take you to all notifications. On the all notifications page, clicking any of the following notification on that page will take you the video, profile, or share associated with that notification.
