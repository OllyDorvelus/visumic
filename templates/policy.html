Policy & Guidelines

Accepted Content

Visumic only allow videos that has to do with creative arts, the four major categories are MUSIC, POETRY, FILM, and DANCE. If an uploaded video has nothing to do with any of these categories then the user will be notified and the following video will be removed. If a video you are about to upload do not fall under any of the genres in the dropdown list then the video does not belong on here. Vlogs are allowed if it has to do with the arts, for example a vlog showing the recording process of a song, music video, film, etc.

Copyright & infringement

Please refrain from stealing material and upload original material. Freestyles from established beats are allowed & covers without the original song playing in the background. Be aware of what you put in your films/web series. Uploaded Stolen Material/Infringement will be notified what is the copyrighted material and then taken down.

Harassment

Any form of HARASSMENT will not be tolerated, three strikes and the account responsible will result into a BAN.

Privacy

Please be mindful of who you share private information with and do not spread other's private information

Spamming

Refrain from being overbearing and repeating the same actions such as commenting spam links that have nothing to do with arts etc

Threats & Speech

Visumic is very tolerant on freedom of speech and won't condem anyone for expressing their point of views but THREATS are off limits and will go by the three strike ban rule.
